about us

The Box Fort.

A writing major, a filmmaker, and a tattoo artist walk into a comic shop. Box Fort Comics was built on a shared love of comics and storytelling. Founded by J. Andrew Calvert, Scott Prather, and W. Scott Calvert, we’re here to make comics that matter—stories with guts, imagination, and intention.

We make the kinds of comics we want to read: bold, heartfelt, mysterious, a little weird, and fully contained. First rule: no endless series. We’re not here to churn out books just to sell them—we all have full-time jobs. And the world doesn’t need more content for content’s sake. We’re here to tell stories. Full stories. Nothing’s worse than investing years of time only to watch a story fall apart at the end (cough Game of Thrones cough). We work hard to respect your investment as a reader. We're not always going to stick the landing, but at Box Fort, we don’t start a story unless we know exactly how it ends.

We’re a small team with different creative interests and backgrounds—spanning art, design, music, film, and more. Each of us is drawn to different kinds of stories, and Box Fort is our place to chase those different passions freely. Whether it’s an all-ages fantasy or something darker and more grown up, our goal is to keep following what excites us—and bring readers along for the ride. The wonder is inside.
