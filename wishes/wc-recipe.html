Drink Recipe

0.5 oz Planteray Stiggins’ Fancy Pineapple Rum
0.5 oz Tito’s Handmade Vodka
0.5 oz Italicus Rosolio di Bergamotto
1 oz Limecello
2.5 oz Blue Coco Syrup

Shake all the ingredients with ice, then strain into a Poco Grande or Gibraltar glass full of ice. Garnish with lime and Blue Raspberry Pop Rocks.

Limecello Recipe

33 g Lime Peels (organic)
210 ml Everclear (190 proof)
380 ml Water
132 grams Sugar

Peel limes being careful to remove the white pith. Combine peels and Everclear in glass jar for several days, agitating 2x per day. Dissolve sugar into water. Strain lime alcohol and combine with sugar water. Bottle.

Blue Coco Syrup

13 oz Chaokoh Coconut Milk
380 ml Water
1.6 oz Licor 43
6.5 oz Hawaiian Shaved Ice Blue Rasperry Syrup
5 g Jolly Rancher Blue Raspberry Powder
13 g Citric Acid
8 g Malic Acid

Mix ingredients until combined. Store in fridge.
