Hiraeth: The Adventures of Bevel and Logan

For Logan, being seven isn’t easy. He’s full of big feelings and even bigger questions. On the day of his grandfather’s funeral, Logan runs away—and stumbles into something impossible: a big-hearted monster, a message of hope, and a strange world of magic and mystery. Along the way, he confronts tricksters, deadly storms, blackhearted lords,  colossal creatures, and eerie childhood nightmares.

Hiraeth is a dark fantasy limited series in the tradition of Don Bluth films, Little Nemo, and Alice in Wonderland: thrilling, funny, and just the right kind of scary. A story about holding on, letting go, and everything in between. Journey beyond goodbye. Step into Hiraeth!

how our shop works

We do things a little differently at Box Fort. Our shop over at Big Cartel only opens a few times a year, and just for a few weeks at a time. Not exactly business-school approved—but it lets us focus most of the year on making the best comics we can. When the shop is open, we pour all our energy into packing orders and getting books into your hands fast. The rest of the year, we’re heads-down making the next issue. If you can't wait, you can catch us at conventions, pop-ups, and comic shop events where we’ll always have books on hand to sell. Check back here to see if the shop is open—or, better yet, sign up for the newsletter below so you never miss a drop.
